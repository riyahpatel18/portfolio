Tools: Excel, Tableau
Skills: Data Cleaning, Dashboarding
Tableau Public Dashboard

Overview

I am an avid lover of escape rooms! I have done at least 15 escape rooms since 2017, all over the United States. I have done multiple escape rooms in Florida, North Carolina, & Massachsuetts. Therefore, I was interested in analyzing escape room data.

I searched for data sets online and was able to find a blog that had created their own report on the Escape Rooms within the United States/Puerto Rico for 2024. The blog also had a escape room directory, containg cities and escape room names so I used those for my dataset.

Data Preparation

I changed "USA" to "US" to have consistent data as some rows had differing values. Used Text to Column and "[" as a delimiter to split types of escape rooms into a column so names could be separate from type of escape room (if it was not a regular escape room business) into a different column.

    Filled in
Filled in blanks with "Regular" for those company names that did not specify that they were a special type of escape room.

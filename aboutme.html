About Me

I'm a data enthusiast with a passion for transforming complex information into actionable insights. With a strong educational foundation from my Master's program and proficiency in Excel, Tableau, and SQL, I approach each data challenge with both technical expertise and creative problem-solving skills. I enjoy identifying patterns, extracting meaningful insights, and presenting data in compelling visualizations that tell a story.

Intellectually curious by nature, I thrive on solving complex problems and uncovering insights that others might miss. This drive fuels my continuous learning and allows me to approach challenges with enthusiasm and persistence.

Beyond the data world, my analytical mindset extends to my personal interests. You can find me solving escape rooms on weekends or watching crime shows, appreciating how investigators use similar analytical methods to connect evidence and solve cases—both data analysis and detective work require attention to detail, pattern recognition, and logical reasoning to uncover hidden truths.

I bring this combination of technical skills, academic knowledge, and analytical thinking to every project, always eager to dive into data and discover the solutions hidden within the numbers. ​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​I created this portfolio to showcase personal projects I am working on to practice and improve my technical skills!
